# GrowthOS — Go To Market (Phase 0)

**The thesis:** merchants are the *subject* of the report, never the buyer.
We sell to the people who make money **from** merchants: ecommerce/CRO agencies
and productized freelancers. The report is the **weapon**, not the product.

**Their ROI:** one report that closes one €3,000/mo retainer = ~€36,000/year.
On a €300/mo tool that's 20–100x, recurring. A merchant's ROI is ~2x, once.

**Phase 0 goal:** 5 agencies paying, by hand, within 14 days.
No product work until that gate is passed.

---

## 1. The ICP (be ruthless)

**Who:** ecommerce / CRO / Shopify agencies and productized freelancers, **1–20 people**,
who sell **retainers** to DTC brands.

**Qualify in:**
- Founder-led (reachable — no procurement, buys on a card)
- Actively selling (posting, cold outreach, running ads = they feel pipeline pain)
- Retainer model (€1.5k–€10k/mo per client)

**Qualify out:**
- Big brand agencies (slow, committee, no pipeline pain)
- Devs/web-builders who don't sell growth outcomes
- Anyone who says "we're at capacity" (no pain = no sale)

**The pain we hit:** *pipeline*. Cold outbound is dead — everyone's inbox is AI spam.
The only thing still getting replies is genuine, specific personalisation. That's
exactly what our tech makes, at zero marginal cost.

## 3. The play (do things that don't scale)

For **each** agency, before you contact them:
1. Find **3 ecommerce stores that look like their ideal client**
2. Run GrowthOS on all 3 → grab the 3 report links
3. Send the agency the 3 leads **free, unsolicited, no strings**
4. Only then, plant the offer

You are not pitching a tool. **You are handing them pipeline.** The tool is the P.S.

## 4. The scripts

### Cold email — first touch

> **Subject:** 3 leads for {Agency} (already did the work)
>
> Hi {Name} — I scanned 3 Shopify stores that look like your kind of client and
> found where each one is leaking revenue:
>
> • **{store1.com}** — hero has no value prop, ~€2.3k/mo leaking → {report link}
> • **{store2.com}** — zero trust signals on product pages, ~€1.8k/mo → {report link}
> • **{store3.com}** — no email capture at all, ~€3.1k/mo → {report link}
>
> They're yours. Send them as-is, put your name on them, I don't want anything.
> If one books you a call I'd genuinely love to hear about it.
>
> — {You}
>
> P.S. I'm building this into a tool that does it for any list of stores,
> white-labeled to your brand. If it books you a client it's €300/mo. If it
> doesn't, don't pay.

**Why it works:** gives before it asks, is unfakeably specific (real stores, real
findings), and the offer is a P.S. — not a pitch.

### DM version (X / LinkedIn)

> Hey {Name} — scanned 3 stores that look like your ICP and found where each is
> leaking revenue. Yours free, no strings: {link}
> Built the scanner myself. If it's useful I'll send more.

### Follow-up — day 3

> Did any of those 3 land? Happy to run 5 more on whatever list you're working —
> just send me the domains.

### Follow-up — day 7 (the ask)

> Straight question: if this booked you **one** extra client a month, would you
> pay €300/mo for it? Yes → I'll set you up today. No → totally fine, tell me why
> and I'll stop.

### The only question that matters

> *"If this booked you a call, would you pay €300/mo?"*

Anything other than a **yes + card** is a no. "Interesting", "keep me posted",
"send me info" = no. Write it down as a no and move on.

## 5. The offer

| Plan | Price | For |
|---|---|---|
| Solo | €99/mo | 1 seat, freelancers |
| **Agency** | **€299/mo** | team seats, **white-label**, volume — *the target* |
| Scale | €799/mo | unlimited, API, integrations |

**Risk reversal:** *"If it doesn't book you a call in 30 days, don't pay."*
You can say that honestly — and it's what makes it closable.

**€10M ARR ≈ 2,800 agencies at €300/mo.** A rounding error of the market.
(Versus ~345,000 one-off €29 sales/year, on a treadmill, with zero retention.)

## 6. Gates & kill criteria

- **Day 14: 5 agencies paying.** Can't get 5 by hand → thesis is dead. Stop. €0 spent learning it.
- **Month 2: 1 agency closes a real client** using our report → that's the case study that unlocks everything.
- **Churn >10%/mo after month 2** → you built a time-saver, not a pipeline tool → reposition or stop.

## 7. What NOT to build until the gate is passed

No dashboard. No AI chat. No competitor mode. No settings. No billing portal.
**Nothing.** Phase 0 is a spreadsheet, a browser, and your hands.

Only after 5 paying agencies, build exactly three things:
1. Bulk scan (paste/upload domains)
2. Auto-generated personalised first-touch message per prospect
3. White-label report link (their logo, their domain)
